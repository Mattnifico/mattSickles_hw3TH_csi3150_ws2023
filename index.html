Moonball: A Popular Earth Pastime with a Lunar Twist

Moonball is a exhilirating game based off of multiple activities popular on earth. A mix of basketball and football with a gravity-defying twist, Moonball involves two teams vying for control of a ball and making it collide with the moon's surface.

How Does It Work?

Moonball starts with two teams of starships taking off from the moon's surface to battle for control of a ball suspended in the air. When one team touches the ball, it turns to that team's color (say if a red team touches the ball, the ball would turn red). Once the ball has turned to a team's color, that team's goal is to get the ball to hit the moon's surface for a point. However, the opposing team can also hit the ball at any point to change it to their color, giving them a point. This means that the game gets increasingly harrowing as the ball gets closer and closer to the moon.
